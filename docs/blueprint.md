# **App Name**: Lumière Graduate

## Core Features:

- Ethereal Glass Hero: A luxury glassmorphism invitation card with 3D parallax movement and an animated opening ceremony featuring digital confetti and golden cap motion.
- AI Sentiment Polish: A tool that uses generative AI to help guests refine their digital wishes and comments into elegant, sophisticated celebratory messages.
- Interactive Achievement Showcase: Floating UI cards displaying academic statistics, degree honors, and key milestones with smooth entrance transitions.
- Seamless RSVP Portal: A clean, integrated form for guests to confirm attendance and dietary preferences, with data persistence and real-time updates.
- Live Ceremonial Ticker: An elegant, cinematic countdown timer that uses dynamic scaling typography to build anticipation for the commencement ceremony.
- Curated Memory Vault: A premium parallax-enabled image gallery and interactive timeline of the graduate's journey from matriculation to graduation.
- Interactive Wishes Hub: A dynamic board for guests to leave greetings and emojis, styled with a soft cinematic blur and glass layers.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette is centered on 'Champagne Achievement', featuring a primary Golden hue (#C5A059) to evoke success and luxury.
- The background uses an Ivory White (#FAF8F5) with low saturation to provide a clean, high-end editorial feel.
- Accent elements utilize a Warm Bronze (#9E6B3F) to create sophisticated contrast for secondary information and iconography.
- Headlines use 'Playfair' for an elegant, high-contrast serif look. Body text uses 'PT Sans' for clear, humanist sans-serif legibility.
- Mobile-first, single-column stacked layout that transitions into a cinematic split-screen desktop experience with wide airy gutters and luxury negative space.
- Fine-lined golden icons with subtle gradients representing academic symbols and logistical directions (location, time, RSVP).
- Fluid, physics-based scrolling and glassmorphism hover effects where cards glow and slightly lift under the cursor movement.
